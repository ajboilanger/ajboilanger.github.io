Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Pumpkin pie! I can't think of a more "fall" dessert than that. Whether you take the time to make it from scratch, or you grab one from the store - no fall holiday seems complete without it (and honestly, any holiday would be better with it in my opinion!)

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) unbaked pie crust
  • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Whipped cream for serving
